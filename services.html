Support Services

At CozyPaws Cafe, we're more than just a cafe. We provide various services to support our feline friends and community.

Explore the comprehensive services we offer at CozyPaws Cafe to support our feline residents, customers, and the wider community. Each category below details our key service areas with specific hours of operation.

  1. Cat Care & Adoption Services

    Our primary mission is to provide exceptional care for rescue cats and facilitate loving adoptions into forever homes.

    • Adoption Counseling: Mon-Fri: 11am-5pm, Sat-Sun: 10am-6pm
    • Cat Health Checks: Daily: 9am-10am & 8pm-9pm
    • Foster Program Support: By appointment only (contact via form)
    • Veterinary Partner Visits: 1st & 3rd Tuesday of each month: 2pm-4pm

  2. Cafe & Event Services

    Enjoy our cafe services and special events designed for cat lovers of all ages.

    • Regular Cafe Hours: Daily: 10am-8pm
    • Cat-Themed Birthday Parties: Weekends: 2pm-4pm & 5pm-7pm
    • Art with Cats Workshops: Every Saturday: 11am-1pm
    • Barista Training (Cat-Friendly): Monthly, first Sunday: 10am-12pm

  3. Community Support

    We partner with local organizations to foster a supportive and engaged community.

    • Pet Food Bank: First Saturday of each month: 12pm-3pm
    • Reading with Cats Program: Tuesdays & Thursdays: 4pm-6pm
    • Anxiety Support Groups: Wednesdays: 7pm-9pm (Therapy cats present)
    • Student Volunteering: Weekday afternoons by prior arrangement

  4. Facility & Maintenance

    Our facility services ensure a safe, clean, and welcoming environment for everyone.

    • Deep Cleaning & Sanitization: Mondays (closed to public): 9am-12pm
    • Supplies & Cat Food Delivery: Wednesdays & Fridays: 8am-10am
    • Equipment Maintenance: Last Sunday of each month: 8am-11am
    • HVAC System Check: Quarterly, as scheduled

Need Special Arrangements or Local Recommendations?

For services outside regular hours or special accommodations, please contact us. We're also happy to recommend trusted local partners for grooming, veterinary care, and other pet needs in the Kittyville area.
