Support Services

At CoxyPaws Cafe, we're more than just a cafe. We provide various services to support our feline friends and community.

  1. Cat Care & Adoption Services

    Our primary mission is to provide care for rescue cats and facilitate adoptions.

    • Adoption Counseling: Mon-Fri: 11am-5pm, Sat-Sun: 10am-6pm
    • Cat Health Checks: Daily: 9am-10am & 8pm-9pm
    • Foster Program Support: By appointment only (contact via form)

  2. Cafe & Event Services

    Enjoy our cafe services and special events designed for cat lovers.

    • Regular Cafe Hours: Daily: 10am-8pm
    • Cat-Themed Parties: Weekends: 2pm-4pm & 5pm-7pm
    • Art with Cats Workshops: Every Saturday: 11am-1pm

  3. Community Support

    We partner with local organizations to support the community.

    • Pet Food Bank: First Saturday of each month: 12pm-3pm
    • Reading with Cats Program: Tuesdays & Thursdays: 4pm-6pm
    • Support Groups: Wednesdays: 7pm-9pm (Anxiety support with cats)

  4. Facility & Maintenance

    Our facility services to ensure a safe and clean environment.

    • Deep Cleaning: Mondays (closed to public): 9am-12pm
    • Supplies Delivery: Wednesdays & Fridays: 8am-10am
    • Equipment Maintenance: Last Sunday of each month: 8am-11am

Need Special Arrangements?

For services outside regular hours or special accommodations, please contact us.
